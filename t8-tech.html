Wonder of Internet

>

09/21/2015

Internet was developed by U.S government. The primary from of internet was used for regional academic and military purpose. Commercialized internet was getting popular since mid 1990. In these days, internet is being widely used for various devices and purposes.

Internet is a network to link devices by using Internet protocol suite. It is being used for putting up website, social networking, sharing files, television, phone, online-banking, ordering items, email, finding direction and etc. Most people owns smart phone, which is a mini computer, and carry around it. Internet has been changing life style a lot.

Internet needs both hardware and protocols to work. Two of the most important protocols could be identified as transmission control protocol (TCP) and the Internet protocol (IP); these two are often grouped together as TCP/IP. Protocol sets up rule for how information would travel. Each devices has its' own IP address. There are two types of IP address, which have been used quite often: IPv4 and IPv6. Because of rising demand of IP addresses, developers built IPv6 to meet the future needs. After transmittng information through TCP/IP protocol, program go thourgh domain name server(DNS). When it reaches to the web server, which is directed by DNS. Request will travel back to the device in the form of packet by hitting DNS and protocols.
